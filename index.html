I love building open source platforms for AI

Over the years, I've spent a lot of time architecting cloud-based, on-premises and hybrid solutions for large corporations all the way down to start-ups. Recently I've helped companies with their AI strategies, platforms and go-to-market execution plans. My philosophy is simple: apply the Lean Start-Up principles to every project I work on. What exactly does that look like?

  • Eliminate uncertainty by testing a vision continuously. Fail fast and fail cheap.
  • Work smarter, not harder.
  • Develop a minimum viable product quickly, measure how customers respond and adjust frequently.
  • Use actionable metrics, milestones and priorities to learn and grow.
I've found that this yields quicker results, validates the product meets customer needs more efficiently, and most of all shows faster return on investment to stakeholders. Everyone is happier with faster ROIs.

My Love of AI

I enjoy driving innovation to solve challenging problems for customers with AI and Machine Learning needs.
